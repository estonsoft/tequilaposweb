Understanding Cash Discount: A Smart Way to Cut Processing Fees

A clear explanation of what a Cash Discount is, how it works, and how it benefits both businesses and customers.

WHAT IS CASH DISCOUNT?

A Cash Discount is a business-friendly pricing strategy where customers receive a discount when they choose to pay using cash, debit card, or ACH (bank transfer) instead of a credit card.

This method is designed to help merchants recover the cost of credit card processing fees, which can range from 2% to 4% per transaction. Instead of increasing prices across the board or adding extra fees for credit card users (which may be restricted or frowned upon), businesses offer an incentive to customers who pay through lower-cost payment channels.

HOW DOES IT WORK WITH A POS SOLUTION ?

01 Two-Tier Pricing Setup

The POS system is programmed to show a standard price (card price) and automatically apply a discount when the customer chooses cash, debit, or ACH payment.

02 Automatic Discount Application

When a customer selects a cash or debit payment option, the POS automatically applies the discount—no manual calculation needed.

03 Receipt Transparency

The receipt clearly shows whether a discount was applied or not, ensuring full visibility and compliance with legal and card network rules.

04 Fully Integrated Workflow

The cash discount feature is built into the POS system, making it seamless for staff and easy for customers—no changes to your existing process.

WHY IS THIS IMPORTANT?

01 Eliminates Credit Card Processing Fees

Businesses can offset or completely remove costly credit card fees, saving thousands annually.

02 Increases Profit Margins

By encouraging lower-cost payment methods, businesses retain more revenue from each sale.

03 Encourages Cost-Effective Payment Behavior

Customers are incentivized to pay with cash or debit, reducing the business’s dependency on card networks.

04 Provides Transparent, Legal Compliance

With the right POS setup, pricing is clearly displayed, and discounts are applied in a compliant, customer-friendly way.
