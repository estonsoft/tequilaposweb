Stop Employee Theft Before It Starts: Tools You Already Have

Turn Everyday Features Into Powerful Theft Deterrents.

POS Features That Detect and Deter Internal Theft

Stay in control of sales, staff, and shrinkage with these essential daily and weekly POS reports.

User permissions allow you to control what each employee can do in the POS system. Not every staff member should have access to sensitive functions like voiding sales, applying manual discounts, or opening the cash drawer without a transaction.

How it helps:

  • •

    Prevents unauthorized discounts or voids.

  • •

    Limits the ability to manipulate transactions.

  • •

    Ensures only managers can approve critical actions.

An audit trail is a detailed log of every action taken in the POS system—who did what, when, and from which terminal. This includes voids, discounts, no-sale drawer openings, deleted items, and more.

How it helps:

  • •

    Provides a clear record of employee activity.

  • •

    Helps identify patterns linked to theft, like repeated voids or excessive discounts.

  • •

    Builds accountability—employees are less likely to steal if they know their actions are tracked.

End-of-shift reports give managers a snapshot of key metrics like total sales, cash collected, number of voids, discounts applied, and drawer over/short amounts. These can be broken down by employee.

How it helps:

  • •

    Makes it easy to spot red flags at the end of each shift.

  • •

    Helps compare staff performance and identify anomalies.

  • •

    Supports quick resolution of cash drawer discrepancies.

Key Reports Every Restaurant Owner Should Review Regularly

From detecting suspicious activity to tightening controls, TequilaPOS gives you the insights and tools to protect every dollar.

Daily Sales Summary

  • • Total sales by payment type (cash, card, online)
  • • Voids, refunds, discounts applied

Cash Drawer Report

  • • Opening and closing balances
  • • Cash over/short discrepancies

Voids and Refunds Report

  • • Lists who performed them and for which items
  • • Flags fake voids or repeated misuse by staff

Discounts & Comps Report

  • • Shows all applied promotions, discounts, or comps
  • • Useful to catch unauthorized or excessive use

Labor Report

  • • Who clocked in/out and total hours worked
  • • Check for buddy punching or early/late punches

Inventory Usage

  • • Compares expected vs. actual usage
  • • Helps flag theft, spoilage, or over-portioning

Product Mix Report (PMIX)

  • • Shows which items sell best or least
  • • Helps detect manipulations in item sales (e.g., overuse of high-margin comped items)

Inventory Variance Report

  • • Tracks actual stock vs. what should remain
  • • Helps identify missing items or theft over time

Payroll Report

  • • Total labor costs and overtime hours
  • • Keeps labor cost in check and detects abuse

Customer Feedback or Review Summary

  • • Tracks complaints, refunds, or issues
  • • Can indicate service problems or suspicious patterns

Shift Summary Reports

  • • Compares shift performance, sales per employee
  • • Useful for spotting underperforming or suspicious patterns
