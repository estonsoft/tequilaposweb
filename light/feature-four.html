Employee Clock-In & Permission Control

Track Attendance & Manage Staff Access with Ease

Understanding What It Is Permission Control

A reliable system to log staff hours and enforce role-based access across your POS platform.

Employee Clock-In & Permission Control is a feature in POS systems that allows staff to log their working hours directly through the system and ensures that only authorized personnel can access specific functions. It helps track attendance, manage shift timings, and control access to sensitive data or operations—like refunds, discounts, or reports—based on roles or permissions.

  • Secure Clock-In

    Employees clock in using a unique PIN, QR code, or fingerprint—preventing buddy punching and ensuring accurate time tracking. Each entry is logged with the exact time and user ID, improving attendance accuracy and payroll reliability.

  • Role-Based Access

    Each employee is given access only to the functions they need. For example, cashiers can take orders and payments, while only managers can issue refunds or view reports. This limits errors, protects sensitive data, and keeps operations secure.

  • Action Logging

    All critical actions—such as voids, refunds, and clock-outs—are automatically recorded with the employee’s ID and timestamp. This creates a clear, auditable trail that improves accountability and helps resolve disputes or errors quickly.

  • Instant Alerts

    Managers receive real-time alerts for sensitive actions like refunds, voids, or opening the cash drawer without a sale. These notifications help spot unusual activity quickly and maintain better control over daily operations.

  • Sensitive Action Alerts

    Actions like refunds, voids, manual labor edits, or unauthorized discounts trigger instant alerts to managers via email or mobile—ensuring quick response to potential misuse or irregularities.

    Common alert triggers include:

    • Voiding tickets
    • Issuing refunds
    • Applying unauthorized discounts

Why it matters

Ensure accurate time tracking and limit access to sensitive functions, reducing errors and misuse.

Reduces Theft & Misuse

By limiting access and tracking every action, the system helps prevent internal theft, unauthorized discounts, and accidental errors—keeping your operations secure.

Improves Time Tracking

Accurate clock-ins and automated logs ensure reliable attendance records, making payroll processing faster, easier, and error-free.

Protects Business Data

Role-based access ensures that only authorized staff can view or perform sensitive actions, keeping your financial and operational data secure.

Builds Accountability

Every action is tied to an individual user, encouraging responsible behavior and making it easy to trace and resolve any issues.

Peace of Mind for Owners

With real-time alerts and complete activity logs, owners can stay informed and in control—even when they’re away from the business.
