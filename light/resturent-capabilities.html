Powerful Capabilities Built for Every Restaurant

From managing tables to tracking inventory — everything you need to run smoothly, all in one system.

Seamless Table & Payment Management

Quickly assign tables, split bills, and handle tips — all from one intuitive system.

  • Manage tables

    Easily view, assign, and rearrange tables in real time. Optimize seating, reduce wait times, and improve flow during busy shifts for a smoother dining experience.

  • Split checks

    Effortlessly divide bills by item, seat, or custom amount. Make group payments simple and flexible—no confusion, no delays.

  • Tips with ease

    Easily add, adjust, or distribute tips during checkout. Support custom tipping and ensure fair, transparent tip handling for your team.

Real-Time Team Coordination

Keep your kitchen, bar, and floor staff perfectly in sync — no delays, no confusion.

  • Sync kitchen

    Send orders directly to the kitchen in real time through a Kitchen Display System or printer. Eliminate delays, reduce errors, and keep prep running smoothly.

  • Bar

    Instantly route drink orders to the bar station as they’re placed. Speed up drink prep, reduce miscommunication, and ensure timely service for every guest

  • Floor in real time

    Give servers instant access to order updates, table status, and payment info. Keep the front-of-house team in sync for faster, more efficient service.

Total Control at Your Fingertips

Monitor sales, track stock, and oversee staff performance — all in one dashboard.

  • Control sales

    Track every transaction in real time with detailed sales reports. Gain insights into performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions to grow your business.

  • Inventory

    Monitor stock levels, ingredient usage, and restock needs in real time. Prevent shortages, reduce waste, and keep your operations running smoothly.

  • Employee activity

    Track shifts, roles, clock-ins, and performance metrics. Improve accountability, optimize staffing, and ensure smooth day-to-day operations.

No Delays, Just Updates

Monitor sales, track stock, and oversee staff performance — all in one dashboard.

  • Update menu items

    Quickly edit item names, descriptions, and availability. Keep your menu fresh and accurate across all devices in real time.

  • Prices on the fly

    Instantly change prices based on demand, time, or promotions. Stay flexible and keep your pricing up to date without interrupting service.

Performance Insights Made Simple

Track server efficiency and get detailed shift reports to improve team productivity.

  • Track server performance

    Monitor individual server metrics such as sales, tips, table turnover, and customer feedback. Identify top performers, support training needs, and improve overall service quality.

  • Shift reports

    Generate end-of-shift summaries with sales totals, tips, payment breakdowns, and staff activity. Simplify closing procedures and maintain clear, accurate records.
