Built-In Intelligence for Modern Restaurants

Smart Tools for Every Restaurant, From Food Trucks to Fine Dining

Advanced Table Management

Easily manage seating with a visual table layout, track table status in real time, and assign servers effortlessly. Reduce wait times, improve staff coordination, and turn tables faster — all from a single screen.

Fast & Flexible Billing

Quickly generate bills with options to split by item, seat, or group. Apply discounts, taxes, and modifiers in seconds — all while keeping the checkout experience smooth for both staff and guests.vaia

Inventory Control & Low Stock Alerts

Track ingredients in real time, get automatic low stock notifications, and avoid running out of key items. Stay in control, reduce waste, and keep your kitchen fully stocked.

Kitchen Display System

Send orders directly to digital kitchen screens, reducing errors and speeding up prep time. No missed tickets, better order accuracy, and a more organized kitchen workflow.

Online Orders Integrated

Manage orders from Swiggy, Zomato, and your own website in one place. No more switching tabs — all orders flow into your POS for faster, error-free service.
