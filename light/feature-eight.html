Advanced Table Management

Smart tools to optimize seating, reduce wait times, and boost table turnover.

Understanding What It Is

Advanced Table Management gives hosts and managers real-time control over seating, reservations, and table status—all from the POS.

Advanced Table Management simplifies floor operations by giving hosts and servers a clear, real-time view of table status—occupied, reserved, or ready to seat. It streamlines guest flow, reduces wait times, and helps staff manage sections efficiently, especially during peak hours.

Why it matters

Ensure accurate time tracking and limit access to sensitive functions, reducing errors and misuse.

Reduces Guest Wait Times

By showing real-time table status, staff can seat guests faster and avoid confusion. This keeps the flow moving smoothly, especially during busy hours, and creates a better first impression.

Improves Staff Coordination

Hosts, servers, and kitchen staff stay in sync with live table updates and section visibility. This reduces miscommunication, speeds up service, and ensures a smoother dining experience for guests.

Increases revenue

Faster table turnover means more guests served per shift. Efficient seating and smoother operations help maximize dining capacity, leading to higher sales without added labor.

Enhances guest experience

Guests enjoy shorter waits, timely service, and a well-organized dining flow. A smooth seating process and attentive staff create a more pleasant and memorable visit.
