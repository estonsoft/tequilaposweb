Smart Inventory Alerts

Track stock in real time and get notified before you run low.

Inventory Tracking & Low Stock Alerts is a built-in POS feature that lets you monitor inventory levels in real time. It automatically updates stock counts with every sale, return, or restock — across all locations. When items run low, the system instantly notifies you so you can reorder before you run out.

Why it matters

Prevent stockouts, reduce waste, and keep operations running smoothly with timely inventory insights.

Improve Efficiency

With real-time inventory updates, staff no longer needs to manually check shelves or backrooms. They can quickly view stock levels from the POS screen, allowing them to focus on customer service, faster order processing, and smoother operations.

Reduce Waste

Accurate inventory tracking helps you avoid over-ordering items that may expire or go unused. By knowing exactly what’s in stock and what’s selling, you can make smarter purchasing decisions and reduce spoilage, especially with perishable goods.

Make Informed Decisions

Access real-time data on stock and sales to reorder smarter, run targeted promotions, and avoid overstocking slow-moving items.
