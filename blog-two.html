Uncovering the True Cost of Card Payments

High processing fees and unclear pricing models have left many restaurants overpaying — without even knowing it.

The Problems

Many restaurants were paying up to 3–4% per card transaction, often without understanding the breakdown. Monthly statements were complex, fee structures weren’t transparent, and owners had little control to reduce costs.

  • • High credit card processing fees
  • • Vague, hard-to-read monthly statements
  • • No way to offset or reduce costs

The Solutions

✓ Cash Discount Program

  • Automatically applies a small fee to non-cash payments
  • Clear discount for cash customers
  • Fully legal and receipt-compliant

✓ Lower Negotiated Card Rates

  • TequilaPOS partners with processors to offer lower, flat-rate fees
  • Transparent, easy-to-read statements
  • Keep current pricing while reducing costs
