treamline all third-party and direct online orders into one unified system.

Understanding What It Is

A unified system that connects online platforms and delivery services directly to your POS for faster, error-free order handling.

Integrated Online & Delivery Orders refers to the seamless connection between your restaurant’s POS system (Point of Sale) and online ordering platforms (like Zomato, Swiggy, Uber Eats, or your own website/app). This integration automatically sends online orders directly into the POS and kitchen system—just like dine-in orders—without the need for manual entry.

Empowering Restaurant Owners

Key Actions You Can Take to Streamline Operations and Drive Growth

  • Track all online orders in one dashboard

    Employees clock in using a unique PIN, QR code, or fingerprint—preventing buddy punching and ensuring accurate time tracking. Each entry is logged with the exact time and user ID, improving attendance accuracy and payroll reliability.

  • View total sales from Grubhub and Uber Eats

    Owners can easily view total sales from platforms like Grubhub and Uber Eats in one place. This helps track performance, compare channels, and make informed decisions without logging into multiple accounts.

  • Monitor fees and commissions from those platforms

    Keep track of fees and commissions from platforms like Grubhub and Uber Eats directly in your dashboard. This transparency helps owners understand true profit margins and manage costs more effectively.

  • Adjust menu availability by platform (e.g., some items only available for dine-in)

    Owners can customize menu availability by platform—offering certain items exclusively for dine-in, pickup, or delivery. This flexibility helps manage kitchen load, control costs, and tailor offerings to each service channel.

Why it matters

Ensure accurate time tracking and limit access to sensitive functions, reducing errors and misuse.

No Manual Entry Errors

Orders flow directly from the customer to the POS, eliminating manual entry errors like wrong items, quantities, or modifiers. This ensures greater accuracy and fewer mistakes reaching the kitchen.

Faster Counter Service

Staff no longer need to re-enter online or third-party orders into the system, speeding up service and reducing bottlenecks at the register or order station.

Quicker Prep & Delivery

As orders go straight to the kitchen display system (KDS) or printer, kitchen staff can start preparing meals immediately—reducing wait times and improving delivery speed.

No Tablet Juggling

Instead of toggling between multiple third-party apps and devices, all online orders are integrated into a single platform—streamlining workflows and reducing stress for front-of-house staff.

Better Guest Experience

With faster order transmission and fewer mistakes, guests receive their meals quicker and exactly how they ordered them, leading to higher satisfaction and more efficient kitchen performance.

Clear Sales Breakdown

Integrated systems automatically categorize sales channels, helping managers track performance, plan staffing, and make informed business decisions based on clear data.
