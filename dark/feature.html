Built-In Security at Every Level

From login to backup, we safeguard your operations with robust security protocols and constant monitoring.

End-to-End Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted the moment it enters the system and remains protected during transfer and storage, ensuring total privacy and security for every transaction.

Two-Factor Authentication

Adds an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification, ensuring only authorized users can access sensitive parts of your POS system.

PCI-DSS Compliance

Our POS follows PCI-DSS standards to securely process, store, and transmit cardholder data—ensuring every payment is protected and audit-ready.

Role-Based Access Control

Assign permissions based on roles—like cashier, manager, or admin—so each user only accesses what they need, reducing security risks.

Compliance Ready

Our POS system adheres to industry standards like PCI-DSS, ensuring secure handling of payment data and meeting legal requirements with confidence.

Secure Cloud Storage

All your data is stored in encrypted cloud servers with automatic backups, ensuring it stays safe, accessible, and protected from loss or breaches.

Automatic Session Timeout

The system auto-logs out inactive users after a set time, preventing unauthorized access and keeping your POS environment secure.

Activity Logs

Track every user action in the system with detailed logs—ensuring transparency, accountability, and quick issue resolution when needed.
