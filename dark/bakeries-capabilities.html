Freshly Baked Tools for Bakery Success

From managing orders to monitoring inventory, get everything you need to run your bakery smoothly.

Order Now or Ahead of Time

Whether it’s for today or tomorrow, accept and organize orders seamlessly.

  • Accept in-store

    Easily handle in-store orders with a fast, intuitive interface designed for quick service. From dine-in to takeaway, every order is processed smoothly and accurately. Keep your operations efficient and your customers happy with seamless in-person transactions.

  • Pre-orders

    Let customers place pre-orders for pickup or scheduled dining with ease. Orders arrive in your system ahead of time, so your team can prepare efficiently and stay ahead during rush hours. It’s a smart way to reduce wait times and improve customer satisfaction.

Pickup Scheduling with Flexible Payments

Set pickup slots and accept deposits or partial payments — all managed through your POS.

  • Set pickup times

    Allow customers to choose their preferred pickup times when placing orders. Your team gets a clear schedule, helping manage prep and reduce crowding. It’s a convenient way to offer timely service and keep operations running smoothly.

  • Partial payments

    Easily accept partial payments for split bills, deposits, or group orders. Whether customers pay in installments or divide the total, your POS handles it seamlessly. It's flexibility that fits real-life situations and keeps transactions hassle-free.

Stay on Top of Today’s Menu

Track inventory for daily bakes, seasonal treats, and limited-time specials — all from your POS.

Track stock of daily bakes and specials

Stay on top of daily bakes and specials with real-time stock tracking. Know exactly what's available, what’s running low, and update your menu as items sell out. Reduce waste, avoid overselling, and keep your offerings fresh and accurate.

Sell More with Stunning Photos

Feature your top items with visuals that attract attention and increase conversions.

Add photos to help sell signature items

Showcase your signature items with mouthwatering photos right on your POS or menu display. Visuals help customers decide faster and boost interest in high-margin dishes. It’s an easy way to drive more sales with eye-catching presentation.

Update Menus with the Seasons

Add or switch items fast — stay fresh, stay relevant.

Manage seasonal menu changes

Easily update and manage seasonal menu changes with just a few clicks. Add or remove items, adjust pricing, and highlight limited-time offers without disrupting your workflow. Keep your menu fresh and relevant all year round.
