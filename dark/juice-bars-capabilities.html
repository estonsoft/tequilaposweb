Smooth Operations for Smoothie Shops

Take orders fast, manage mix-ins, and track inventory — all from one powerful POS.

Flexible Modifiers for Every Mix

Let customers choose their add-ons — from protein powders to extra toppings — all with easy-to-use modifier tools.

Build custom modifiers (protein, toppings, boosters)

Create custom modifiers to match your menu's flexibility — from proteins and toppings to boosters and extras. Easily add choices to any item, so staff can build orders to exact customer preferences. It’s the perfect way to offer variety without slowing things down.

Effortless Combos & Upcharges

Bundle items, set add-on prices, and boost sales with easy-to-build combo options.

  • Create combos

    Easily build combo deals that bundle popular items together for better value and faster ordering. Whether it’s a drink, side, and main, or any custom pairing, combos streamline the menu and boost upselling. A simple way to increase sales and customer satisfaction.

  • Upcharges easily

    Apply upcharges effortlessly for extras, substitutions, or premium options. Whether it’s an added topping, specialty milk, or size upgrade, pricing adjusts automatically. Keep billing accurate and transparent without slowing down service.

Loyal Customers, Bigger Orders

Offer personalized rewards and automatic upsell prompts to grow retention and revenue.

  • Offer loyalty

    Keep customers coming back with built-in loyalty programs that reward every visit or purchase. Easily set up points, discounts, or exclusive perks to match your brand. It’s a simple way to build stronger relationships and boost repeat business.

  • Upsell suggestions

    Boost your average order value with smart upsell suggestions built into your POS. Prompt staff with add-ons, upgrades, or popular pairings at just the right moment. It’s an effortless way to increase revenue while enhancing the customer experience.

Streamlined Ordering That Speeds Up Service

Take orders faster, minimize delays, and keep the line moving — even during your busiest hours.

Reduce wait times with streamlined ordering

Speed up service and reduce wait times with a streamlined ordering system. Orders flow directly from the counter to the kitchen or barista station, minimizing delays and errors. Keep operations smooth and customers satisfied, even during peak hours.
