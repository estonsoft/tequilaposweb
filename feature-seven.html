Fast & Easy Menu Navigation

Access live sales, staff, and inventory data—anytime, from any device.

Understanding What It Is

A simplified menu layout that helps staff quickly find and select items, modifiers, and combos—reducing order time and boosting efficiency.

Fast & Easy Menu Navigation refers to a streamlined way for restaurant staff—especially servers and cashiers—to quickly browse, search, and select menu items on a POS (Point of Sale) system. It’s designed to reduce order entry time, minimize mistakes, and improve the customer experience.

  • Organized by clear categories (e.g., Tacos, Appetizers, Drinks)

    Menus are organized into clear categories like Tacos, Appetizers, and Drinks, so staff can quickly locate items without scrolling endlessly. This layout streamlines navigation and speeds up the ordering process.

  • Large, easy-to-read buttons with item name and price

    Large, easy-to-read buttons display the item name and price clearly, helping staff place orders quickly with minimal errors—especially in fast-paced environments.

  • One-tap item selection

    With one-tap item selection, staff can quickly add items to an order without extra steps. This simplifies the ordering process and saves valuable time. It’s especially helpful during peak hours when speed and accuracy matter most.

  • Automatic prompts for required modifiers (e.g., toppings, sizes)

    Automatic prompts for required modifiers—like toppings or sizes—ensure staff never miss important details. The system guides them through each selection step-by-step. This reduces errors and keeps orders consistent with customer preferences.

  • Visual feedback when items are added to the ticket

    Automatic prompts for required modifiers—like toppings or sizes—ensure staff never miss important details. The system guides them through each selection step-by-step. This reduces errors and keeps orders consistent with customer preferences.

  • Simple flow from item selection to payment

    The system provides a smooth flow from item selection to payment, making checkout fast and easy. Staff can complete orders quickly without confusion.

Why it matters

Ensure accurate time tracking and limit access to sensitive functions, reducing errors and misuse.

Reduces order time per table

The fast, user-friendly interface reduces order time per table, allowing staff to serve more guests efficiently. This leads to quicker turnarounds and increased table turnover.

Helps new staff learn the system fast

The intuitive design makes it easy for new staff to learn the system quickly. Clear menus and guided prompts reduce training time and boost confidence from day one.

Minimizes errors during high-volume service

During busy hours, the system helps minimize errors by guiding staff through clear, step-by-step workflows. This ensures accurate orders, even under pressure.

Improves overall speed and accuracy at the point of sale

By streamlining the ordering process, the system boosts both speed and accuracy at the point of sale. Staff can take orders faster with fewer mistakes, leading to better service and happier customers.
